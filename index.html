About

What is AuthGuard?

AuthGuard is an all-in-one AI detection platform built to help individuals, journalists, educators, and businesses verify the authenticity of digital content. As AI-generated media becomes increasingly realistic, our suite of tools provides the critical layer of trust needed in today's digital landscape.

Our Detection Tools

Voice Detection

Uses deep learning models trained on thousands of real and synthetic speech samples. Upload any audio clip or record directly — our AI analyzes spectral patterns, pitch variations, and micro-artifacts invisible to the human ear.

Image Detection

Detects AI-generated faces and deepfake images by analyzing pixel-level inconsistencies, lighting anomalies, and generative fingerprints left by tools like Stable Diffusion, MidJourney, and DALL·E.

Text Detection

Identifies AI-written content from models like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Analyzes writing patterns including sentence structure, vocabulary distribution, perplexity scores, and burstiness.

Video Detection

Frame-by-frame deepfake analysis combined with audio track verification. Detects face-swaps, lip-sync manipulations by examining temporal consistency and facial landmark stability.

Social URL Scanner

Paste any Twitter or Reddit link and instantly scan linked content for AI-generated media. Automatically extracts images, text, and audio from social posts and runs them through our detection pipeline.

Authentic Watermark

Protect your original content with an invisible cryptographic watermark. This tamper-proof digital signature proves ownership and authenticity without altering visual or auditory quality.
