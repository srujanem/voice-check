About

What is AuthGuard?

AuthGuard is an all-in-one AI detection platform built to help individuals, journalists, educators, and businesses verify the authenticity of digital content. As AI-generated media becomes increasingly realistic, our suite of tools provides the critical layer of trust needed in today's digital landscape.

Our Detection Tools

Voice Detection

Uses deep learning models trained on thousands of real and synthetic speech samples. Upload any audio clip or record directly — our AI analyzes spectral patterns, pitch variations, and micro-artifacts invisible to the human ear.

Image Detection

Detects AI-generated faces and deepfake images by analyzing pixel-level inconsistencies, lighting anomalies, and generative fingerprints left by tools like Stable Diffusion, MidJourney, and DALL·E.

Text Detection

Identifies AI-written content from models like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Analyzes writing patterns including sentence structure, vocabulary distribution, perplexity scores, and burstiness.

Video Detection

Frame-by-frame deepfake analysis combined with audio track verification. Detects face-swaps, lip-sync manipulations by examining temporal consistency and facial landmark stability.

Social URL Scanner

Paste any Twitter or Reddit link and instantly scan linked content for AI-generated media. Automatically extracts images, text, and audio from social posts and runs them through our detection pipeline.

Authentic Watermark

Protect your original content with an invisible cryptographic watermark. This tamper-proof digital signature proves ownership and authenticity without altering visual or auditory quality.

Document Verification

Scan PDFs, invoices, and official documents for AI-generated or manipulated content. Detects forged signatures, altered text, and synthetic document layouts with forensic-level precision.

Batch Processing

Upload multiple files at once and scan them in parallel. Ideal for newsrooms, content teams, and enterprises that need to verify large volumes of media quickly and efficiently.

Real-time API

Integrate VoiceCheck's detection engine into your own applications with our RESTful API. Get instant JSON responses with confidence scores, metadata, and detailed analysis breakdowns.

Browser Extension

Right-click any image, video, or text on the web and instantly check if it's AI-generated. Our Chrome extension brings deepfake detection directly into your browsing experience.

Scan History & Analytics

Track all your past scans with detailed logs, exportable reports, and trend analytics. Monitor detection patterns over time with a beautiful interactive dashboard.

Multi-Language Support

Detect AI-generated text across 30+ languages including English, Spanish, Hindi, Mandarin, Arabic, and more. Our NLP models are trained on diverse multilingual datasets for global accuracy.

Integrate AI detection into your app in minutes.

# Detect AI voice clone
import requests

response = requests.post(
    "https://api.authguard.ai/v1/voice",
    headers={"Authorization": "Bearer YOUR_API_KEY"},
    files={"audio": open("sample.wav", "rb")}
)

result = response.json()
# { "is_ai": true, "confidence": 0.984, "model": "ElevenLabs" }
print(result["confidence"])
// Detect AI-generated image
const formData = new FormData();
formData.append('image', imageFile);

const res = await fetch('https://api.authguard.ai/v1/image', {
  method: 'POST',
  headers: { 'Authorization': `Bearer ${API_KEY}` },
  body: formData
